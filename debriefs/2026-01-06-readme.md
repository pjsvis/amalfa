# Debriefs

Reflective documents capturing learnings from completed work.

## What is a Debrief?

A **debrief** is written immediately after completing a brief. It captures:

- What worked (successful approaches)
- What failed (dead ends, mistakes)
- Lessons learned (generalizable insights)
- Open questions (future investigation)

## Purpose

Convert experience into explicit knowledge. Debriefs feed into playbooks.
